About Dr. Umran Ali

Qualifications

  • Ph.D: Computer & Video Games (2016)
  • SFHEA: Senior Fellow (Higher Education Academy) (2016)
  • PG Cert: Higher Education (Distinction) (2008)
  • M.A: Creative Technology (Distinction) (2005)
  • BSc: Computer & Video Games (1st Class) (2004)

Dr. Umran Ali is an award-winning educator and pioneering researcher at the intersection of Generative AI, creative technology, and social justice. His decades-long practice in virtual environment design—codified in his seminal 'Virtual Landscapes' book series and demonstrated in commercial video games and interactive projects—provides the foundation for his academic work. This expertise now drives his current research into Generative AI, including the development of practical tools and contributions to best-selling books on level design and digital economies.

Dr. Ali’s career is defined by transformative, evidence-based leadership. As a Programme Leader, he engineered a remarkable turnaround in student satisfaction, leading to a BAFTA award for his students' work. He later forged high-profile partnerships with national broadcasters like the BBC as Director of Creative Media. His influence on academic standards is sought globally, with extensive experience as an External Advisor and Examiner for institutions across the UK and internationally. This standing as a national authority is affirmed by his role as Chair for the Landscape Institute's 'Excellence in Visualization and Digital Practice Award,' where he sets the benchmark for innovation in the field.

Underpinning all of Dr. Ali’s work is a deep and sustained commitment to equality and social justice. This is demonstrated through nearly two decades of institutional leadership, from serving on strategic governance panels like the Race Equality Charter to frontline advocacy. His impact is both practical and profound, exemplified by his leadership of the Remembering Srebrenica Symposium, which used immersive technology to combat genocide denialism and earned the National Award for Best Educational Event.
