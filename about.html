About Dr. Ali

Dr. Umran Ali is an accomplished academic with a career spanning from freelance 3D artistry to his current role as a Senior Lecturer in Arts, Media & Creative Technologies. He is a distinguished Fellow and Senior Fellow of the Higher Education Academy (FHEA, SFHEA), reflecting his commitment to excellence in teaching and scholarship.

Qualifications

  • Ph.D: A Practice Based Exploration of Natural Environment Design in Computer & Video Games (2016)
  • Postgraduate Certificate in Higher Education Research & Practice (Distinction) (2008)
  • M.A Creative Technology (Distinction) (2005)
  • BSc Computer & Video Games (1st Class) (2004)

Previous Appointments

  • 2013-Present: Senior Lecturer in Arts Media & Creative Technologies (UoS)
  • 2011-2013: Director Of Creative Media (UoS)
  • 2009-2011: Senior Lecturer in Creative Media (UoS)
  • 2006-2009: Lecturer in Multimedia (UoS)
  • 2004-2006: Visiting Lecturer in Arts & Media (UoS)
  • 1998-2001: Freelance 3D Artist & Designer
