Dr Umran Ali

Academic • Researcher • Leader

I use an industry and research informed approach to design compelling educational experiences.

An accomplished academic leader with extensive experience in curriculum design, programme management, and research in computer and video games. I have a proven track record of enhancing student satisfaction, leading institutional reviews, and fostering interdisciplinary collaborations. I am also a dedicated trade union representative with significant contributions to university policy, workload management, and staff welfare.
